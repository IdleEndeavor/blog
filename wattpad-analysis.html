A necessary caveat

This is the “most attractive” composite only in a narrow sense: these are the traits that show up most often in the books readers have rewarded with the most reads. That is fictional preference, not real-world preference. Readers of these books are mostly women, mostly young, and the genre is built on a specific kind of fantasy that benefits from clear power asymmetries and emotional arcs that resolve cleanly.
